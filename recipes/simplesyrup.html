Simple Syrup

Simple syrup is a liquid sweetner used in so many drinks you can make at home and it's super easy to make with two simple ingredients! It works great in cold drinks when sugar on it's own may not dissolve very well. Give it a go and up your drink making skills at home!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup water

Directions

  1. Combine your sugar and water to a saucepan over medium heat and bring to a boil. Be sure to stir until the sugar has dissolved.
  2. Allow to cool.
  3. Pour into a clean jar and seal with a tight-fitting lid. You can store the syrup in the fridge for up to a month if properly sealed.
