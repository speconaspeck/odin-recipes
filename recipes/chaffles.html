Chaffles Recipe

Chaffles are delicious and super easy to make. All you need is a waffle maker, cheese, and an egg. Their simplicity makes them super versatile as well. You can eat them as-is or make them part of a larger meal. You can also get creative and start adding other fun ingredients to your chaffles.

Ingredients

  • 1 large egg
  • 1/2 cup shredded mozzarella cheese

Directions

  1. Preheat your waffle maker.
  2. Whisk the egg in a bowl while stirring in the mozzarella cheese.
  3. Pour 1/2 of the batter into your waffle maker. Cook until it steams and is browned to your liking.
  4. Reapeat.
